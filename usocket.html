Release

Latest version of USOCKET-UDP is 2.6, released on September 24, 2010. Changes in this version:

  • Fixed working with USOCKET trunk (0.5),
  • Fixed SOCKET-CONNECT for SBCL (merge from upstream),
  • Adjust +max-datagram-packet-size+ from 65536 to 65507.
